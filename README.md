### üß± Tier 2: The Laddered Shield (Months 4‚Äì24)

## ‚ùì FAQ
1. **Why 24 months? Isn't that excessive?**
Most "legacy" advice suggests 3‚Äì6 months. However, in an AI-driven economy where entire industries can shift overnight, 24 months provides more than just a safety net‚Äîit provides the "time leverage" needed to retrain, pivot, or innovate without the pressure of immediate survival.

2. **How does this protect against inflation?**
By using the Tiered Laddering approach, only the first 3 months stay in a standard liquid account. The remaining 21 months are placed into multiple smaller Fixed Deposits (FDs). This allows the majority of your shield to earn interest and offset "inflation decay" while remaining accessible in small, manageable blocks.

3. **Why should 16-year-olds have their own shield?**
True psychological safety requires sovereignty beyond relationships. Relationships and family dynamics can be volatile; having an independent shield ensures that a young adult's foundation is not tied to the stability or the choices of another person. It instills the discipline of self-reliance early.

4. **What happens if I have to "break" a Fixed Deposit?**
Because the shield is split into multiple small FDs (e.g., 1-month expense blocks), you only liquidate exactly what you need. You only lose the interest on that specific small portion, while the rest of your 24-month foundation continues to earn interest undisturbed.

5. **Can I help family or friends using my shield?**
Yes, but only according to the Smart Withdrawal Protocol. You must prioritize your own survival first; never drop your personal shield below 12 months to help others. Treat any withdrawal for others as a gift in your heart to maintain your own psychological peace.

6. **How does this relate to the Golden Runway?**
They are synergistic. While the Golden Runway is a framework for employers to provide a compassionate offboarding experience and an "Innovation Warranty," Liquid Cash 24 is the individual's personal responsibility to ensure they are never at the mercy of a single employer or market shift.

7. **How do I start?**
Focus on Tier 1 (3 months) first, then gradually build your FD ladder.
